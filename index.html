About Us

Transforming Academic Research at ABU & Beyond

ABU SmartSurvey was created to solve a persistent challenge in Nigerian university research — the difficulty of finding quality, targeted respondents. We bridge the gap between researchers who need data and students who want to contribute to knowledge.

Our Mission

To accelerate academic research by connecting researchers with verified, willing participants across Nigerian universities.

Our Vision

A thriving ecosystem where every researcher can access quality data and every student's contribution to science is recognized.

Our Values

Integrity, academic excellence, community, and the belief that good data drives better decisions and stronger research.
